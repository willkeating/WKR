Current Projects

Here’s a glimpse at some of the machines I’ve built, tuned, and brought back from the dead. Each one tells its own story.

2011 Subaru WRX

The 2011 WRX has a 20G-TDO6, ID1000's, FMIC, Tial BOV, and a Tial 38mm wastegate. The car is tuned by me using cobb accesstuner.

2005 Audi A4

This car was purchased with the ecu cut out of it, a broken window, and no keys. the harness was repaired, I purchased new(used) ECUs for it and adapted them accordingly. I recieved skc logins with some internet tools and a vag kkl. Once that was all done the car did not move and needed a clutch. subsequently a clutch was put in it and its been running since.

1991 RM125

This bike ran for a total of 25-35 minutes post purchase. after disassembly and inspection, many many issues were found. the crank assembly was junk. top end was junk. the crank was allegedly balanced and rebuilt. the cylinder was nikasil'd due to damage. we will find out if it explodes again soon.
